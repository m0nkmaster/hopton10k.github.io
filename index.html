When is it?

Sunday in October 2024, at 10am. Given the time of year, depending on the weather, there may be a few muddy patches!

The route

The route has some short challenging climbs and includes woodland trails, bridleways, footpaths and quiet roads in and around the small village of Hopton, Mirfield.

Why have we created the Hopton 10k?

The woods and fields around Hopton are a great place to run offering some great variety. There are steep climbs, woodland glades, bridleways, and great views over the calder valley. We thought it would make a great place to hold an annual race. So grab your mucky trainers and get involved!
