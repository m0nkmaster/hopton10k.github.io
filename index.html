Multi-terrain run in Mirfield

We've aimed to make the Hopton 10k enjoyable with lots of variety, have great views, and offer a nice gentle introduction to trail running.

The route

The route is somewhat hilly and over mixed terrain. The run takes place on quiet roads, bridleways and footpaths in and around the small village of Hopton, Mirfield.

Why have we created the Hopton 10k?

The woods and fields around Hopton are a great place to run, offering some great variety. There are steep climbs, woodland glades, bridleways, and great views over the calder valley. We thought it would make a great place to hold an annual race; so grab your dirty trainers and get involved!
