Hopton 10k

The mixed terrian 10km race great for the adventurous road runner

The idea

If you've mostly done road racing before but fancy getting your trainers a little bit dirty, then this is the event for you.

Where is it?

Hopton 10k is a new race taking in the lovely rolling hills around Hopton, Mirfield, in the Calder Valley near Huddersfield.

The Course

Fairly hilly and mixed terrain. Taking place on quiet roads, bridleways and footpaths. Roughly 40% road, 40% farm track and 20% path.
