About the Race

Why run the Hopton 10k?

The woods and fields around Hopton offer the perfect trail running experience: steep climbs that test your legs, peaceful woodland glades, ancient bridleways, and panoramic views over the Calder Valley. Whether you're chasing a PB or just looking for a fun, friendly race, grab your trail shoes and join us. All proceeds support Hopton Primary School.

  • UK Athletics licensed event
  • Chip timed with live results
  • All proceeds to Hopton Primary School
  • Finishers T-Shirt for all participants
