Sponsorship Opportunities

Partner with the Hopton 10k

Looking for a unique opportunity to connect with hundreds of active, community-minded people in West Yorkshire? The Hopton 10k offers excellent visibility for local and national businesses alike.

All sponsorship funds go directly to supporting Hopton Primary School. To discuss options, use the contact form below or email info@hopton10k.run.

Package 1: Platinum sponsor (£800) – 1 package available

In return for your support as platinum sponsor, Hopton 10k would provide:

  • Presenting sponsor i.e. “The Hopton 10k brought to you by (Your Company Name Here)”
  • Your company logo in priority position and size on the Hopton 10k T-shirt (awarded to all runners)
  • Your company logo on the Hopton 10k website with a link to your company website
  • A shout out on the Hopton 10k race Facebook page with a link to your website
  • A space at the event to host a display table / promotional stand (provided by sponsor)
  • Acknowledgement on the day over the race PA system
  • Opportunity to place promotional merchandise or materials in the venue
  • Consecutive event sponsorship available (you will get first refusal on next year’s event)
  • Primary sponsor mention and thank you in the event recap on website and Facebook
  • 10 x FREE entries to the race.

Package 2: Standard T-shirt sponsor (£400) – 6 packages

In return for your support in sponsoring the t-shirts, Hopton 10k would provide:

  • Your company logo on back of race t-shirts, beneath the main sponsor
  • Your company logo on the Hopton 10k website with a link to your company website
  • Acknowledgement on the day over the race PA system
  • Opportunity to place promotional merchandise or materials in the venue
  • A shout out on the Hopton 10k race Facebook page/twitter account with a link to your website
  • Mention and thank you in the event recap on website and Facebook
  • 3 x FREE entries to the race.

Package 3: Race number sponsor (£200) – 1 package available

In return for your support in sponsoring the race numbers, Hopton 10k would provide:

  • Your company logo on the race numbers
  • Your company logo on the Hopton 10k website with a link to your company website
  • Acknowledgement on the day over the race PA system
  • Opportunity to place promotional merchandise or materials (provided by sponsor) in our finisher’s goody bags
  • A shout out on the Hopton 10k race Facebook page/twitter account with a link to your website
  • Mention and thank you in the event recap on website and Facebook
  • 2 x FREE entries to the race.

Custom Sponsorship

Ultimately, the options above can be adjusted, and we welcome your suggestions. We are extremely eager for local businesses and large national companies alike to get involved. We offer a unique opportunity for companies to gain exposure to hundreds of runners, as well as the local community. If you are interested in reaching individuals who are passionate about sports, fitness, and community involvement, we believe our race presents an excellent opportunity. Moreover, we are raising money solely for the school. All organization and support on race day is provided entirely by dedicated volunteers.

The Hopton 10k is an annual trail race through the beautiful woods and fields around Hopton, West Yorkshire. All proceeds support Hopton Primary School.
