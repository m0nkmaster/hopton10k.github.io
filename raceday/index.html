Everything you need to know to make your race day run smoothly. For general questions about entering, see our FAQ page.

Getting There

Location

The race starts and finishes at Hopton Primary School, Woodend Road, Mirfield, WF14 8PR.

Important: Enter the school grounds through the playground gate on Chadwick Lane. The main reception and front gates will not be open.

For sat nav, use: Chadwick Lane, WF14 8PN

Parking Options

As the school is in a residential area, please consider these options:

Option A: Hopton New Road (Recommended) Hopton New Road is just south of Mirfield Train Station, about a 5-minute walk from the school. Space for approximately 70 cars. Usually quiet on Sunday mornings.

Option B: School Playground Chadwick Lane, Mirfield, WF14 8PN. Approximately 60 spaces available.

Option C: Lower Hopton Club 53 North St, Mirfield, WF14 8PN. Approximately 20 spaces available.

We recommend trying Hopton New Road first, then heading to the school if no spaces are available.

Please consider car-sharing or public transport where possible.

Race HQ Facilities

At the school you’ll find:

  • Registration desk (in the school hall)
  • Baggage storage (locked classroom, staffed during race)
  • Unisex changing room
  • Toilets (mobile classrooms + main school building)
  • Cafe serving Tea and coffee and snacks
  • First aid support (First+ Medical Solutions)

Collecting Your Race Number

  1. Head straight into the school hall (don’t queue outside)
  2. Find the registration desk for your surname initial
  3. Tell us your name and collect your race number
  4. Attach your number to your front (safety pins provided)
  5. Drop your bag at the cloakroom if needed

Tip: Arrive early to avoid the last-minute rush. Registration closes at 9:50am.

The Route

The route leaves the school via the playing field gates, heading down Woodend Road. Runners follow Calder Road, Granny Lane, and Hagg Lane before entering the woods and running clockwise through the trails. The route then returns to the school.

Key features:

  • Mix of road, trail, and bridleway
  • Water station at the 5km mark (Ouzelwell Lane, Thornhill Lees)
  • Some steep climbs (especially after the water station!)
  • No toilets on route

At the Finish

When you cross the finish line, you’ll receive:

  • Your finishing time (results posted to RaceBest shortly after the race)
  • A technical running T-shirt (the size you selected when entering)
  • A goody bag with treats
  • Water

Hang around for tea, coffee, and to cheer in fellow runners!

Important Notes

Medical Information

Please declare any pre-existing medical conditions when you register and display this information clearly on your race number. Three medics from First+ Medical Solutions will be stationed around the course, with a treatment centre at the start/finish.

If you’re feeling unwell, please don’t run.

Photography

Volunteer photographers will be at various points around the course. All photos will be available free of charge on the Hopton 10k website a day or two after the race.

T-Shirt Sizes

You’ll receive the size you selected when entering. If you need to change size, hang around until the end to see if there are any left - we always have a few dropouts so you may be lucky. We cannot post T-shirts - if you’re not there on the day, you won’t receive one.

Questions?

Still have questions? Get in touch and we’ll help you out.

The Hopton 10k is an annual trail race through the beautiful woods and fields around Hopton, West Yorkshire. All proceeds support Hopton Primary School.
